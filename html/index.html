Beech Lane Farmhouse

The Farmhouse

Open floor plan includes great kitchen with appliances, dining and living room. Tiled or timber flooring throughout. Gas stove in sitting area. Economic electrical heating throughout. Very well insulated so great saving on those high heating bills! Broadband everywhere in the house. Large master bedroom with a full bath en-suite. Another double bedroom with shower en-suite. The house also has wheelchair access and a wet room. Entire property fenced in, front and back. A great place for children to play safely and watch the animals and the work going on in the fields.
