A Historical City

It'll blow your mind.

Kilkenny is filled with historical beauty that dates back as far as the 12th century. Kilkenny Castle, Woodstock gardens, Jerpoint Abbey, St. Canices Cathedral and Jenkinstown Park are just a few of he many historical sites around. There is beautiful scenery to be seen at all of these locations. Mount Juliet is also famous for its beautiful scenery. All of these sites are no more than a short drive from Beech Lane accomodation.

Facilities in KilkennySee for yourself.

Visit the Watershed for a fun filled activity session to keep your fitness levels up on the running track or in the swimming pool. See the front desk for further information. An outdoor swimming pool in Bagenalstown, Kiltorcan Go Carting, the Set Theatre and the Watergate Theatre, Gowran Reptile Zoo, Castlecomer Discovery Park, amazing Golf courses including Mount Juliet, horse racing in Gowran, crafts and walks are a few of the possibilities for getting the most out of your holidays.
